    Cement and Concrete Applications #

    Impulse excitation testing is valuable for characterizing cementitious materials, providing essential information about their mechanical properties, quality, and performance characteristics.

    Overview #

    Cementitious materials are complex composite materials requiring:

    • Property characterization - Understanding material behavior
    • Quality assurance - Ensuring consistent performance
    • Process optimization - Improving manufacturing processes
    • Performance validation - Verifying material performance
